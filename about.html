Dafi Scholarship

The AFI Conservatory offers a limited number of scholarships each year to both domestic and international incoming Fellows. Scholarships are awarded based on financial need and merit. Scholarship award amounts vary and may partially cover the cost of tuition and fees. Several scholarships are available for Fellows from underrepresented communities, specific nationalities and Conservatory disciplines.

We are a social impact enterprise re-skilling thousands of refugees into digital professionals to deliver sales, marketing, recruitment, fundraising and SEO for individuals, start-ups, scale-ups and Enterprises. We believe access to education and opportunities is a fundamental human right, yet 80 million refugees do not have these rights. Our vision is to reskill and employ 1 million refugees in 1 marketable skill over the next ten years. You can join us in this human revolution where the global community comes together to address the refugee crisis in the most dignified way.
